# ISS Expedition 74 Crew Kicks Off Week With Cygnus Cargo Training and Spacewalk Cleanup

**摘要：** 国际空间站第 74 远征队乘组开始为诺斯罗普·格鲁曼 Cygnus XL 货运飞船的到来进行训练，维护上周太空行走使用的宇航服，并开展了紧急医疗演习。

## Cygnus XL Cargo Mission Preparation

**Expedition 74** astronauts are preparing for the arrival of **Northrop Grumman's Cygnus XL** cargo spacecraft, targeted for launch **no earlier than April 8 at 8:49 a.m. EDT**. NASA flight engineers **Chris Williams** and **Jack Hathaway** reviewed the upcoming mission, trained for rendezvous procedures, and familiarized themselves with monitoring equipment.

Cygnus will deliver advanced microgravity research gear to study:

- Quantum physics in microgravity
- Stem cell therapies
- Astronaut health monitoring systems

## Post-Spacewalk Spacesuit Servicing

NASA flight engineer **Jessica Meir** spent Monday servicing the spacesuits she and Williams wore during a **7-hour, 2-minute spacewalk on March 18** (EVA 94). She:

- Cleaned and flushed the suit's water-cooling loops that regulate body temperature
- Emptied and refilled the water system to remove gas bubbles and contaminants

## Science and Operations

**ESA** flight engineer **Sophie Adenot** focused on multiple scientific experiments:

- Collecting data from an investigation studying ways to quickly download large amounts of data from space
- Swapping research samples inside a science freezer
- Inventorying biomedical gear used for cardiovascular health studies
- Recording a video message for students describing the importance of space science

## Emergency Drill

All four astronauts joined **Roscosmos** cosmonaut **Andrey Fedyaev** for a mid-afternoon emergency medical drill, reviewing:

- Locations and use of medical hardware
- CPR (cardiopulmonary resuscitation) procedures
- Individual roles during medical emergencies in low Earth orbit

## Roscosmos Segment Activities

- **Andrey Fedyaev**: Replaced smoke detectors and gas masks in the Nauka science module; checked drinking water quality
- **Sergey Kud-Sverchkov** (Station Commander): Serviced electrical components and checked the Elektron oxygen generator in Zvezda service module; studied AI tools for improving crew operations
- **Sergei Mikaev**: Offloaded supplies from **Progress 94** resupply ship that docked March 24
