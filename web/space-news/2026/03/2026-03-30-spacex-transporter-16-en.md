# SpaceX Set to Launch 119 Payloads on Transporter-16 Rideshare Mission From Vandenberg

**摘要：** SpaceX 将于 2026 年 3 月 30 日使用猎鹰 9 号从范登堡太空军基地执行 Transporter-16 拼车任务，携带 119 个有效载荷进入轨道。

## Mission Overview

On **March 30, 2026**, SpaceX will launch the **Transporter-16** rideshare mission aboard a **Falcon 9** rocket from **Vandenberg Space Force Base** in California. The mission will carry **119 payloads** to sun-synchronous orbit and other orbits, continuing SpaceX's commitment to affordable access to space for small satellite operators.

The launch window opens at **6:20 AM EDT** (**10:20 UTC**).

## Mission Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Launch Vehicle | Falcon 9 |
| Launch Site | Vandenberg SFB (SLC-4E) |
| Launch Time | March 30, 6:20 AM EDT (~10:20 UTC) |
| Payload Count | 119 |
| Orbit | Sun-synchronous orbit |

## NASA Payloads Onboard

NASA is flying multiple technology and science demonstrations aboard Transporter-16, supported through various industry collaborations. These payloads will test new capabilities in low Earth orbit, ranging from advanced communications to materials science experiments.

## Booster Recovery

Following stage separation, the Falcon 9 first stage will attempt a landing at Vandenberg's Landing Zone or on a droneship in the Pacific Ocean, continuing SpaceX's track record of booster reuse.
