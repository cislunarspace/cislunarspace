# China launches Tianlian II-04 data-relay satellite

**Summary:** Per China’s government English portal and follow-on coverage, at **23:55 Beijing Time on March 26, 2025**, a **Long March-3B** rocket launched **Tianlian II-04** from the **Xichang Satellite Launch Center**, Sichuan. The satellite is a second-generation **geosynchronous data-relay** spacecraft to support TT&C and relay services for crewed spacecraft, space stations, and selected LEO missions. The flight was the **565th** mission of the **Long March** launch vehicle family.
