# SpaceX Starlink launch from Vandenberg

**Summary:** Per *Space.com* coverage of the **March 26, 2025** flight, **SpaceX** launched a **Starlink** batch on **Falcon 9** from **Vandenberg Space Force Base**, California, and recovered the **first stage** on a droneship at sea (article references a **Group 11-7**-related label; confirm on SpaceX mission pages).
