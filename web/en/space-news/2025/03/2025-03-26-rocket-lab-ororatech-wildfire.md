# Rocket Lab launches eight OroraTech wildfire satellites

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reported **Rocket Lab**’s **Electron** launch from **Mahia, New Zealand** on the **“Finding Hot Wildfires Near You”** mission, deploying **eight** **OTC-P1** satellites for **OroraTech** (Germany) into **LEO** (article cites **~550 km** altitude, **~97°** inclination). Liftoff timing is described relative to **UTC on March 26, 2025** and local **March 27** NZ time; Rocket Lab confirmed deployment of all eight spacecraft. OroraTech aims for up to **~100** satellites by **2028** for global wildfire monitoring.
