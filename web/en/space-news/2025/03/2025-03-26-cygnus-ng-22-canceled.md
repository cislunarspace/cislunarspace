# NASA and Northrop cancel Cygnus NG-22 cargo mission

**Summary:** According to *SpaceNews*, **NASA** and **Northrop Grumman** stated **March 26, 2025** that the **Cygnus NG-22** cargo mission to the **ISS**, previously targeted for **June**, will not fly as planned because the **pressurized cargo module** was **damaged in transit** from supplier to Northrop. The company said it is assessing schedule effects and working to support **NG-23** **no earlier than fall 2025** to deliver cargo safely.
