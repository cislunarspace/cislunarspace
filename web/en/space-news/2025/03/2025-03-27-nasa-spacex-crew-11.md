# NASA announces SpaceX Crew-11 crew assignments

**Summary:** NASA release **25-010** (**March 27, 2025**) names the **SpaceX Crew-11** crew: **Commander Zena Cardman** (NASA), **Pilot Mike Fincke** (NASA), **Mission Specialist Kimiya Yui** (JAXA), and **Mission Specialist Oleg Platonov** (Roscosmos). They are to launch **no earlier than July 2025** for a long-duration expedition on the **ISS** under the **Commercial Crew Program**—the **11th** operational crew rotation with **SpaceX**.
