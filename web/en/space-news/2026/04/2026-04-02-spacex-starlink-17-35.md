# SpaceX Starlink 17-35 launch from Vandenberg

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reports the **Starlink 17-35** mission launched on **April 2, 2026**, at approximately **23:03 UTC** on a **Falcon 9** from **Space Launch Complex 4E**, **Vandenberg Space Force Base**, California, carrying **25** Starlink V2 Mini Optimized satellites. Booster **B1103** made its ** maiden flight** (brand new booster) and landed on the droneship **"Of Course I Still Love You."**
