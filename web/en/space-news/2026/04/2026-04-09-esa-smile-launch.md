# ESA-CAS Smile mission launch

**Summary:** According to **ESA**, the **Smile** (Solar wind Magnetosphere Ionosphere Link Explorer) mission is scheduled to launch on **April 9, 2026, at 08:29 CEST** (06:29 UTC) on a **Vega-C** rocket from the **European Space Centre in French Guiana**.

**Mission Overview:**
Smile is a joint mission between **ESA** and the **Chinese Academy of Sciences** designed to study how Earth responds to the **solar wind**, improving understanding of solar storms, geomagnetic storms, and space weather phenomena.

**Launch Preparations:**
- March 24: Smile completed fuel loading
- March 26: Pre-launch press conference
