# Chang'e 7 Probe Arrives at Wenchang Launch Center for Planned 2026 Launch

**Summary:** As of the evening of April 9, 2026, the Chang'e 7 probe has safely arrived at the Wenchang Spacecraft Launch Site, with plans for a launch in the second half of this year. The mission will achieve breakthrough technologies including high-precision lunar soft landing, legged walking, lunar surface flight, and exploration of permanent shadow craters.

> The safe arrival of the Chang'e 7 probe marks significant progress in China's lunar exploration program, laying a solid foundation for future crewed lunar missions.

## Mission Overview

The Chang'e 7 mission is one of the most important tasks in China's deep space exploration, possessing significant scientific value and technical importance.

### Scientific Objectives
The main objectives of the Chang'e 7 mission include:
- **High-precision Lunar Soft Landing**: Breakthrough precision landing technology
- **Legged Walking**: Enable autonomous movement on the lunar surface
- **Lunar Surface Flight**: Carry out 3D exploration with aerial vehicles
- **Permanent Shadow Crater Exploration**: Explore the environment and resources in lunar south pole permanent shadow areas

### Exploration Method
The mission will adopt a **comprehensive exploration method combining orbit, landing, survey, and flight** to conduct all-round exploration of the lunar south pole region's environment and resources.

### Technical Breakthroughs
The Chang'e 7 mission will achieve several key technological breakthroughs:
- **High-precision Landing Technology**: Achieve millimeter-level precision lunar landing
- **Lunar Surface Mobility**: Design and control of legged walking mechanisms
- **Lunar Flight Technology**: Takeoff, landing, and flight of small aerial vehicles on lunar surface
- **Extreme Environment Detection**: Special environment detection in permanent shadow craters

## Mission Progress

### Launch Preparation Status
- **Probe Status**: Safely arrived at Wenchang Spacecraft Launch Site through air-land transport
- **Launch Site Status**: Facilities and equipment in good condition, testing preparations proceeding as planned
- **Preparation Progress**: Related preparation procedures being carried out in an orderly manner with comprehensive launch preparations underway

### International Cooperation
The Chang'e 7 mission will also carry out relevant international cooperation during implementation, making full use of international aerospace resources to enhance the scientific content and international influence of the exploration mission.

## Significance

### Technological Level
The technological breakthroughs of the Chang'e 7 mission will provide important technical support for future crewed lunar landing missions:
- **Landing Technology Verification**: High-precision landing is one of the core technologies for crewed lunar landing
- **Lunar Surface Mobility Capability**: Accumulate experience for lunar base construction
- **Resource Exploration Technology**: Lay the foundation for lunar resource development

### Scientific Level
Through comprehensive exploration of the lunar south pole region, the Chang'e 7 mission will:
- **Reveal Lunar Evolution History**: Through geological analysis of permanent shadow craters
- **Search for Water Ice Resources**: Provide important resource guarantee for future lunar bases
- **Conduct Environmental Monitoring**: Establish a foundation for long-term lunar scientific observation

## Future Planning

The Chang'e 7 mission is an important part of China's lunar exploration program "three-step" strategy, promoting the comprehensive enhancement of China's deep space exploration capabilities.

### Resource Integration
To fully leverage the advantages of the new national system model, China will deeply integrate resource forces in the existing crewed lunar landing and unmanned lunar exploration fields:

- **Technology Integration**: Integrate technical capabilities accumulated over decades from crewed spaceflight and Chang'e programs
- **Experience Integration**: Use previous lunar exploration practical experience to enhance exploration benefits
- **Resource Integration**: Optimize resource allocation to form exploration synergy

### Mission Continuity
The Chang'e 7 mission will be closely connected with subsequent crewed lunar landing missions, forming a complete lunar exploration technology chain:

- **Unmanned Lunar Exploration**: Chang'e 7 and other unmanned exploration missions paving the way for crewed landing
- **Crewed Lunar Landing**: Accumulate experience and technology for subsequent crewed landing missions
- **Lunar Base**: Lay the foundation for future long-term lunar presence and development

## Progress of China's Lunar Exploration Program

The Chang'e 7 mission is an important milestone in China's lunar exploration program, marking the entry of China's lunar exploration into a new stage of development.

### Lunar Exploration Achievements
- **Chang'e 4**: First-ever soft landing on the far side of the Moon
- **Chang'e 5**: Lunar sample return mission
- **Chang'e 6**: Far side lunar sample return mission
- **Chang'e 7**: Comprehensive lunar south pole exploration

### Technology System Perfection
Through the implementation of the Chang'e series missions, China has established a complete lunar exploration technology system:
- **Rocket Technology**: Continuous improvement of Long March rocket reliability
- **Probe Technology**: Increasing maturity of landers, rovers, and orbiters
- **Tracking and Control**: Expanding coverage of deep space tracking networks
- **Recovery Technology**: Verification of lunar sample return technology

The successful implementation of the Chang'e 7 mission will further enhance China's technical strength and international influence in the international deep space exploration field, making new contributions to peaceful use of outer space and exploration of the mysteries of the universe.
