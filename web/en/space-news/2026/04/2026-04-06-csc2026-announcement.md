# 2026 China Space Conference (CSC2026) to be Held in Chengdu on April 23-26

**Summary:** The China Space Conference, co-hosted by the Chinese Society of Astronautics and the China Space Foundation, is a comprehensive industry event with widespread influence in China's aerospace sector.

![China Space Conference](./figures/2026-04-06-csc2026-announcement/hero.jpg)
*Credit: Chinese Society of Astronautics* Founded in 2018, the "2026 China Space Conference (CSC2026)" will be held in Chengdu from April 23-26, 2026. Themed "Seventy Years Exploring the Heavens, Journeying the Nine Realms Together," the conference stands on the glorious 70-year journey of China's aerospace and the new journey of the "15th Five-Year Plan." It will invite renowned experts, scholars, and managers from domestic and international aerospace fields to discuss the blueprint for high-quality aerospace development, comprehensively showcase frontier aerospace technology achievements, and deepen pragmatic cooperation in the international aerospace field.

## Conference Theme and Significance

This conference is themed **"Seventy Years Exploring the Heavens, Journeying the Nine Realms Together,"** which holds multiple important meanings:

- **Standing on the glorious 70-year journey of China's aerospace**: 2026 marks the 70th anniversary of the founding of China's aerospace industry, and the conference will comprehensively review and showcase China's aerospace achievements over the past 70 years
- **New journey of the "15th Five-Year Plan"**: Standing at the starting point of the "15th Five-Year Plan," discussing China's aerospace development direction and strategic layout for the next five years
- **Deepening pragmatic cooperation in international aerospace**: Inviting renowned experts, scholars, and managers from domestic and international aerospace fields to promote international aerospace exchanges and cooperation

## Conference Time and Location

**Hosted by:** Chinese Society of Astronautics, China Space Foundation

**Time:** April 23-26, 2026

**Location:** Chengdu Century City New International Convention and Exhibition Center, Century City International Conference Center, Sichuan University, etc.

## Conference Activity System

The conference includes **academic, industrial, science popularization, and cultural innovation** activity systems and will host **more than 30 diverse activities**, fully covering the following key areas:

- Space Science
- Space Technology
- Space Applications
- Space and Society

## Background of China Space Conference

The China Space Conference (CSC) was founded in 2018 and has been successfully held for multiple sessions, becoming a comprehensive industry event with widespread influence in China's aerospace sector. Held annually, the conference provides an exchange and cooperation platform for experts, scholars, and industry professionals from domestic and international aerospace fields, comprehensively showcasing China's aerospace development achievements and promoting aerospace technology innovation and industrial progress.

2026 marks the 70th anniversary of the founding of China's aerospace industry, and this conference holds important historical significance, injecting new momentum for China's aerospace innovation and development during the "15th Five-Year Plan" period.
