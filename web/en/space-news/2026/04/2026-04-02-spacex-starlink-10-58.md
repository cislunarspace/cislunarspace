# SpaceX Starlink 10-58 launch from Cape Canaveral

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reports the **Starlink 10-58** mission launched on **April 2, 2026**, at approximately **11:52 UTC** on a **Falcon 9** from **Space Launch Complex 40**, **Cape Canaveral Space Force Station**, Florida, carrying **29** Starlink V2 Mini Optimized satellites. Booster **B1085** flew its **15th** mission and landed on the droneship **"Just Read the Instructions."**
