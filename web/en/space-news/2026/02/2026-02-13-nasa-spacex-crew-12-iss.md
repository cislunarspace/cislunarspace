# NASA’s SpaceX Crew-12 launches to the International Space Station

**Summary:** NASA’s International Space Station blog reports that **NASA’s SpaceX Crew-12** lifted off at **5:15 a.m. EST on Friday, Feb. 13, 2026**, from **Space Launch Complex 40** at **Cape Canaveral Space Force Station**, Florida. The Dragon spacecraft carried **NASA astronauts Jessica Meir (commander) and Jack Hathaway**, **ESA astronaut Sophie Adenot**, and **Roscosmos cosmonaut Andrey Fedyaev**. The spacecraft was expected to take about **34 hours** to autonomously dock to the station’s **Harmony** module. This is the **12th** crew rotation mission of SpaceX’s Dragon and Falcon 9 under NASA’s **Commercial Crew Program**. Launch photos appear on the NASA pages linked below.
