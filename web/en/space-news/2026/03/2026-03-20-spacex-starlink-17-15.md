# SpaceX Starlink 17-15 launch from Vandenberg

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reports the **Starlink 17-15** mission launched on **March 20, 2026**, at **2:51:49 p.m. PDT** (**21:51:49 UTC**) on a **Falcon 9** from **Space Launch Complex 4E**, **Vandenberg Space Force Base**, California, carrying **25** Starlink V2 Mini Optimized satellites. Booster **B1100** flew its **4th** mission and landed on the droneship **"Of Course I Still Love You."**
