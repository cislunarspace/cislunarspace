# Artemis II SLS rocket rolls out to launch pad

**Summary:** According to *Spaceflight Now*, on **March 19, 2026**, NASA rolled the **SLS (Space Launch System)** rocket from the **Vehicle Assembly Building** to **Launch Pad 39B** at Kennedy Space Center, a journey of approximately **12 hours**. **Artemis II** is NASA's first crewed lunar orbit test mission, planned for launch **no earlier than April 1**, with crew members NASA astronauts Reid Wiseman (commander), Victor Glover, Christina Koch, and Canadian astronaut Jeremy Hansen.

The rocket was returned to VAB on February 21 due to hydrogen leak and upper stage propulsion helium issues. The Flight Readiness Review was completed on March 12.
