# SpaceX Starlink 10-44 launch from Cape Canaveral

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reports the **Starlink 10-44** mission launched on **March 29, 2026**, at approximately **5:15 p.m. EDT** (**21:15 UTC**) on a **Falcon 9** from **Space Launch Complex 40**, **Cape Canaveral Space Force Station**, Florida, carrying **29** Starlink V2 Mini Optimized satellites. Booster **B1067** flew its **34th** mission, set to break the Falcon booster reuse record, and landed on the droneship **"Just Read the Instructions."**
