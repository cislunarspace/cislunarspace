# Shiyan-30 03 & 04 satellites launched

**Summary:** According to **China National Space Administration (CNSA)**, the **Long March 2D** rocket launched **Shiyan-30 03 & 04 satellites** on **March 13, 2026**, at **06:33 Beijing Time** from the **Xichang Satellite Launch Center**. The satellites are used for earth observation technology testing and verification. This was the **632nd** flight of the Long March series rockets.
