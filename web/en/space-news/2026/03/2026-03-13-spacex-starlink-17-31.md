# SpaceX Starlink 17-31 launch from Vandenberg

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reports the **Starlink 17-31** mission launched on **March 13, 2026**, at **6:57:59 a.m. PDT** (**13:57:59 UTC**) on a **Falcon 9** from **Space Launch Complex 4E**, **Vandenberg Space Force Base**, California, carrying **25** Starlink V2 Mini Optimized satellites. Booster **B1071** flew its **32nd** mission and landed on the droneship **"Of Course I Still Love You."** Payload deploy occurred about one hour after liftoff.
