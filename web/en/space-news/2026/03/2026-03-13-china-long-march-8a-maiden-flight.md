# Long March 8A maiden flight success

**Summary:** According to **China National Space Administration (CNSA)**, the **Long March 8A** rocket made its successful maiden flight on **March 13, 2026**, at **03:48 Beijing Time** from the **Hainan Commercial Space Launch Site**, delivering **20 satellite internet LEO satellites** to orbit. Long March 8A is developed by **China Academy of Aerospace Launch Vehicle Technology (CALT)** and is a two-and-a-half stage liquid rocket.
