# NASA unveils $20 billion lunar base plan

**Summary:** According to *Spaceflight Now*, on **March 24, 2026**, NASA announced a **$20 billion** lunar base plan over seven years to establish a semi-permanent presence on the Moon. The plan has three phases:
- **Phase 1**: Increase lunar landing frequency, deliver rovers, instruments, and technology payloads
- **Phase 2**: Develop habitat modules and infrastructure
- **Phase 3**: Permanent infrastructure

The plan includes nuclear and solar power systems, lunar GPS, and other technologies. The "Skyfall" Mars mission is planned for 2028.
