# Rocket Lab launches Strix Earth observation radar satellite

**Summary:** According to *Space.com*, on **March 20, 2026**, **Rocket Lab's Electron** rocket successfully launched the **Strix satellite** from **Launch Complex 1, Mahia, New Zealand**, at 2:10 p.m. EDT (1810 GMT, 7:10 a.m. local New Zealand time on March 21). Strix is an **Earth observation radar satellite** for Japanese company **Synspective**.
