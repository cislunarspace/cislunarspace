# SpaceX Starlink 10-33 launch from Cape Canaveral

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reports the **Starlink 10-33** mission launched on **March 19, 2026**, at **10:20 a.m. EDT** (**14:20 UTC**) on a **Falcon 9** from **Space Launch Complex 40**, **Cape Canaveral Space Force Station**, Florida, carrying **29** Starlink V2 Mini satellites. Booster **B1077** flew its **27th** mission and landed on the droneship **"Just Read the Instructions."** This was SpaceX's **588th** booster recovery.
