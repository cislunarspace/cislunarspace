# China launches Yaogan-50 02 remote sensing satellite

**Summary:** Xinhua, as carried by CCTV.com English, reports that on **Mar. 15, 2026**, at **9:22 p.m. Beijing Time**, a **modified Long March-6** rocket launched the **Yaogan-50 02** satellite from the **Taiyuan Satellite Launch Center**, Shanxi Province. The satellite entered its planned orbit; the mission was a complete success. The spacecraft is used mainly for **land survey, crop yield estimation, and disaster prevention and relief**. This was the **633rd** flight of the **Long March** launch vehicle family. Mission photography credited to **Shang Yuhang/Xinhua** appears on the CCTV page below.
