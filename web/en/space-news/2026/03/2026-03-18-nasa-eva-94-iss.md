# NASA astronauts complete ISS US EVA 94

**Summary:** According to **NASA**, **US EVA 94** was conducted on **March 18, 2026**, beginning at approximately **8:00 a.m. EDT**, lasting about **6.5 hours**. NASA astronauts **Jessica Meir** (her 4th spacewalk) and **Chris Williams** (his first spacewalk) completed tasks to prepare for the installation of Roll-Out Solar Arrays (IROSA) for the 2A and 3B power channels.
