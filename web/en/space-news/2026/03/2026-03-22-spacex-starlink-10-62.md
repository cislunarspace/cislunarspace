# SpaceX Starlink 10-62 launch from Cape Canaveral

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reports the **Starlink 10-62** mission launched on **March 22, 2026**, at **10:47 a.m. EDT** (**14:47 UTC**) on a **Falcon 9** from **Space Launch Complex 40**, **Cape Canaveral Space Force Station**, Florida, carrying **29** Starlink V2 Mini Optimized satellites. Booster **B1078** flew its **27th** mission and landed on the droneship **"A Shortfall of Gravitas."** This was SpaceX's **590th** booster recovery.
