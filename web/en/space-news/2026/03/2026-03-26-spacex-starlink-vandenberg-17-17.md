# SpaceX Starlink 17-17 launch from Vandenberg

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reports the **Starlink 17-17** mission launched on **March 26, 2026**, at **4:03:19 p.m. PDT** (**23:03:19 UTC**) on a **Falcon 9** from **Space Launch Complex 4E**, **Vandenberg Space Force Base**, California, carrying **25** Starlink satellites. Booster **B1081** flew its **23rd** mission and landed on the droneship **“Of Course I Still Love You.”** Payload deploy occurred a little over **one hour** after liftoff. The flight had slipped two days from **March 24** for unspecified vehicle/payload reasons.
