# SpaceX Starlink 10-46 launch from Cape Canaveral

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reports the **Starlink 10-46** mission launched on **March 17, 2026**, at **9:27 a.m. EDT** (**13:27 UTC**) on a **Falcon 9** from **Space Launch Complex 40**, **Cape Canaveral Space Force Station**, Florida, carrying **29** Starlink V2 Mini Optimized satellites. Booster **B1090** flew its **11th** mission and landed on the droneship **"A Shortfall of Gravitas."** This was SpaceX's **587th** booster recovery.
