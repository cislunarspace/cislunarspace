# SpaceX Starlink 17-24 launch from Vandenberg

**Summary:** *Spaceflight Now* reports the **Starlink 17-24** mission launched on **March 16, 2026**, at **10:19:09 p.m. PDT** (**March 17, 06:19:09 UTC**) on a **Falcon 9** from **Space Launch Complex 4E**, **Vandenberg Space Force Base**, California, carrying **25** Starlink V2 Mini Optimized satellites. Booster **B1088** flew its **14th** mission and landed on the droneship **"Of Course I Still Love You."**

**Milestone:** This launch coincided with the **100th anniversary** of Robert Goddard's first liquid-fueled rocket launch (March 16, 1926), and SpaceX's Starlink constellation exceeded **10,000 satellites** in orbit simultaneously. This was SpaceX Falcon 9's **615th** flight.
