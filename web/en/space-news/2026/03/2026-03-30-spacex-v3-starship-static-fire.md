# SpaceX Fires Up Next-Gen V3 Starship for First Time Ahead of April Launch

**摘要：** SpaceX 于 2026 年 3 月完成了新一代 V3 版星舰的首次静态点火测试，为 4 月发射做准备。

## V3 Upgrades

The **V3 Starship** represents a major design upgrade over previous versions, featuring:

- **Greater thrust**: Enhanced Raptor engines delivering higher performance
- **Increased payload capacity**: Optimized structural design for heavier payloads to orbit
- **Improved thermal protection**: Enhanced heat shield for atmospheric reentry

## Static Fire Test

SpaceX successfully completed the first static fire test of the V3 Starship at its Starbase facility in Boca Chica, Texas. Static fire testing is a critical milestone that involves igniting the engines while the vehicle remains secured to the launch mount, validating propulsion and vehicle systems before flight.

## April Launch Target

SpaceX CEO Elon Musk has indicated the V3 Starship's inaugural flight is expected in **April 2026**. This mission will test the new design improvements and demonstrate the vehicle's orbital capabilities.

## Significance

The Starship program is central to SpaceX's long-term ambitions, including serving as NASA's Artemis lunar lander and enabling future Mars missions. The V3 iteration pushes the platform closer to full operational capability.
