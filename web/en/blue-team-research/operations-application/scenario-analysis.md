# Scenarios & Evidence

## Scenario bounds

Each scenario states time span, geography, parties, and space-related factors; all must come from **citable open** material.

## Evidence chain

List official statements, press, and academic analysis in order; flag timing and decay; present contradictions side by side with reasons for preference.

## Conclusions

Separate **facts**, **reasonable inference**, and **cannot be judged from open sources**—do not state speculation as fact.
