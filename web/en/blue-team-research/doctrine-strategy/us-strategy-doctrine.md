# U.S. Strategy & Doctrine System

## Scope

Official guidance stresses that blue-team work must grasp how adversary action rests on **doctrine**. This topic is dedicated to aligning public **strategic** statements with **issued doctrine** for U.S. (and openly allied) sources, anchoring equipment and operations analysis.

## Sources (open only)

- Public national defense and security strategy products, annual defense reports;
- Public CJCS, service, and functional-component doctrine summaries or official interpretations;
- Open congressional testimony and legislative material on space and joint operations.

## Requirements

Build an index of document, version, and intended scope; distinguish strategic messaging from actionable doctrine. Quotes must trace to page or paragraph.
