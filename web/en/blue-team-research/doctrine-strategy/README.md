# Doctrine & Strategy · Overview

## Content

This track traces how adversary space operations, force development, and command relationships are described in **public** texts, linking doctrine, strategy, and policy for use by equipment and operations tracks. See [U.S. strategy & doctrine system](./us-strategy-doctrine) for the dedicated deep dive on doctrine-grounded action.

Hosted PDF mirrors for *Space Force Doctrine Document 1* (open materials) are listed under [Public doctrine PDFs](./us-strategy-doctrine#sfdd1).

## Sources

Official publications, departmental and service sites, congressional reports, and published doctrine summaries or scholarly paraphrases—each with full citation.
