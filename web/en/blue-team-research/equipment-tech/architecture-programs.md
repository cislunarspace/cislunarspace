# Architectures & Programs

## Layering

When mapping public texts to strategic, operational, and tactical support layers, list **role statements, owning agencies, and citations** for each layer—avoid inferring entire architectures from single articles.

## Program timelines

Rows are programs or systems; columns are time; milestones use only **publicly released** contract or announcement text with date and link.

## Doctrine cross-check

Major capability milestones are traced back to doctrine or strategy statements; note agreement, lag, or gaps.
